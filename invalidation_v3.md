# Project Reactor Core v3 — Thesis Invalidation Checklist
*Run this quarterly, after major macro events, or when you feel the urge to deviate.*
*Price moves alone are not invalidation. Ask: has reality changed, or just price?*

---

## How to Use This

For each position, answer the questions. If you hit an invalidation trigger:
- **Yellow** (1 trigger): Watch closely. Do not act yet. Re-run checklist in 30 days.
- **Red** (2+ triggers, or 1 marked CRITICAL): Thesis is broken. Size down or exit.

Mark each review with a date. Decisions made without this checklist are emotional.

## When NOT to Run This Checklist

- After a -10% month in the portfolio. Price is not thesis.
- When you read a bearish article about one of the positions.
- When a position is down and others are up (rebalance, don't exit).

Run it when: earnings reveal structural problems, macro data fundamentally shifts, or a competitor achieves something that changes the supply/demand math.
